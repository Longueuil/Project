Program Overview

The Computer Networking and Technical Support diploma program provides you with strong technical and interpersonal skills - both are important professional skills that will give you an advantage as a technical support specialist in the computer industry.

Networking and Technical Support professionals research, analyze and recommend strategies for network architecture and development. Companies around the world need them to implement, manage, maintain and configure network hardware and software. They also monitor and optimize performance, in addition to troubleshooting and providing end-user support. Their primary objective is to ensure that companies suffer no hiccups in their networking and data security needs.

Industries across the career spectrum and around the world depend on Networking and Technical Support professionals to keep employees connected and business flowing. This makes it a necessary part of any large company and for this, the job outlook is very bright. This diploma aims to put your foot into this evolving world of cloud computing and virtualization.

The diploma in Networking and Technical Support aims to transfer industry-relevant and job-oriented skills to the participants to enable them integrate into the workforce and become an asset in the work environment. The program is designed to allow participants to gain intermediate to expert level competencies in a range of technical area.

- Installation, configuration and maintenance of computer components and peripherals

- Troubleshooting, repair and maintenance of computer components, connectivity issues, viruses and malware

- Client and server operating systems and networking fundamentals

- Build Virtualization and cloud infrastructure
