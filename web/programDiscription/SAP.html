Program Overview

The SAP Supply Chain Management diploma program provides you with a solid grounding in both business and global supply chain issues. This program is delivered with the integration of ERP technology (SAP) in the course ware giving you the technical skills to integrate technology into business processes.

SAP Supply Chain Management professionals plan and manage the flow of organizational products systematically to meet customer service goals, optimize inventory investment and meet production requirements. Their primary goal is to ensure the continuity of the supply of goods while minimizing costs.

Today, successful companies rely on the skills of SAP Supply Chain Management professionals to keep their goods and services flowing to the marketplace quickly, efficiently, and as cost-effectively as possible. SCM is a bright spot among up-and-coming careers, with employment opportunities in a wide variety of industries, and in firms of all sizes.

The SAP Supply Chain Management diploma is delivered in a constructive model. The course would begin with a theoretical topic preparing the students on the basic aspects of Supply Chain Management. Several job description will be presented to help the students identify areas of focus and interest. The next phase of the course will have a scenario based teaching on the SAP Supply Chain Management platform where students will learn the concepts directly in the tool. This approach will prepare the students to analyze industry situations and equip them with adequate concepts to implement such scenarios in SAP. The students have the opportunity to interact among themselves and the instructor to analyze different ways of solving the problem.
