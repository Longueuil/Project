Program Overview

The Montreal College’s Digital Marketing & Web development AEC Program will provide you with a complete digital marketer expertise with the combination of Front-end Web Development useful for Digital Marketing Professionals. This program covers marketing domains, such as Search Engine Optimization, Social Media, Pay-per-Click, Conversion Optimization, Digital Analytics, Content, Mobile, Marketing automation and Web development.

Demand for Digital Marketing with combination of Front end Developers is widespread across every industry, and continues to rise. By mastering the skills taught in this program, you’ll be prepared for roles at a wide array of companies, from startups to global organizations. The projects you’ll build, and the portfolio you’ll develop will build the confidence to be Job Ready.

The program offers instructor-led classes conducted by multiple expert trainers. . This course will help you to:

-Develop an in-depth understanding of Digital Marketing functions such as Inbound Paid and Social media marketing and Web Analytics.

-Develop skills in planning, managing, and implementing Campaigns.

-Understand how Digital marketing disciplines all work together and how to optimize your use of each one.

-Lead Marketing teams and initiatives for your organization.

-Build and maintain websites, able to create landing pages, email marketing and marketing automation campaigns.
